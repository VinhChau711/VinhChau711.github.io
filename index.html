I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Statistics at the University of Michigan, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Jonathan Terhorst and Professor Long Nguyen. Before coming to University of Michigan, I was graduated as the valadictorian of Hanoi University of Science, class of 2019.

Research interests

A central theme of my research focuses on:

  • Hierarchical Models: Identifiability, Statistical Efficiency, and Model Selection Methods

  • Optimal Transport

  • Functional Data Analysis

  • Theoretical Probability/Statistics: Convergence theory, Stein-Chen method
